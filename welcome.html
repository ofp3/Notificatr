Welcome to an\note.

How it works:

an\note is a simple, in-browser note taking application. It is designed to let you write notes in a clean, well-formatted, and highly readable way.

The an\note engine is focused around tags. A tag, denoted by a backslash (\) marks special parts of your notes. There are tags for equations (\eq...\eq*), theorems (\thm...\thm*), and many other useful types of information. Anything enclosed in tags can be searched for later and the an\note engine will extract only the information that you are looking for.

In addition, an\note has full markdown support. This means that any markdown style formatting you do will be automatically transferred to the html version of your notes, which can be both saved and printed.
